Site Purpose

The purpose of this site will be to help rock climbers plan their next rock climbing trips. Right now, there are apps to help you find the route, but there aren't any websites or applications that I know of that help a user just have a general guide to a rock climbing area. The only things I know of are books for specific places, and I want to bring that to the web!
