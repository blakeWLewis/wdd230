🙋🏼‍♂️ Target Audience

Description of target audience

My target audience is local business owners in Cottonwood Heights who promote not only their business but their city and the activities they have surrounding their city.

Two personas

The two personas I want to be coming to this website are people thinking of moving to Cottonwood Heights and starting a business and current business owners in Cottonwood Heights. I also would like to have heads of charities and other people involved in their community come to this site and see other people that are involved and basically create a small network of a community.

Scenarios

The scenarios that I think someone will come to my website are
1. Business owners want to attend and advertise at events.
2. Business Owners wanting to give back to their community
3. Someone may want to see upcoming events
4. Help people understand the demographics and statistics of the city
5. Join and be more involved in the community
