Population

With 32,864 people, Cottonwood Heights is the 33rd most populated city in the state of Utah out of 329 cities. But watch out, Cottonwood Heights, because Clearfield with 32,238 people and Holladay with 31,390 people are right behind you.

Race & Ethnicity

The largest Cottonwood Heights racial/ethnic groups are White (88.4%) followed by Hispanic (5.0%) and Two or More (2.6%).

Median Income

In 2020, the median household income of Cottonwood Heights households was $95,427. Cottonwood Heights households made slightly more than Huntsville households ($95,000) and Lindon households ($95,233) . However, 0.0% of Cottonwood Heights families live in poverty.

Median Age

The median age for Cottonwood Heights residents is 39.0 years young.
