Originally founded in 1976 Jordan Bountiful was a farmer in the great city of carlsbad california when he had an idea. His idea was to make a company that started to sell smoothies as well as custom smoothies to people along the southern coast of califonia. While all companies start out small Jordan’s company recieved a large amount of positive feedback. Jordan started to be known as the smoothie king due to his smoothies being so fresh and local grown many we’re drawn to the company. His company helped bring in new revenue opportunities to the company.
